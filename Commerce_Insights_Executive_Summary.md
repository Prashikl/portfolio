# Commerce Insights — Executive Summary

## What I Worked On
Commerce Insights is an enterprise seller‑facing platform that aggregates complex commerce data across renewals, subscriptions, concessions, L+SA, and lifecycle milestones.

My work focused on transforming Commerce Insights from a **reporting dashboard** into a **trusted, action‑driven decision system**.

---

## The Problem
Despite rich data, sellers struggled to:
- Prioritise what needed action now
- Trust the data enough to act
- Make decisions without exporting to Excel

The core issue was not data availability — it was the absence of **judgement encoded into the experience**.

---

## The Design Shift
I reframed Commerce Insights around three principles:

1. **Decision over reporting**  
   Prioritised signals and next actions instead of exhaustive metrics.

2. **Trust as a UX requirement**  
   Surfaced data freshness, source context, and confidence cues to enable safe action.

3. **Speed to decision**  
   Reduced time‑to‑decision by highlighting deltas, risk, and change — not raw values.

---

## Impact (Design → Outcome)
- Reduced reliance on offline analysis (Excel)
- Faster identification of at‑risk renewals
- Increased confidence to act directly within the tool
- Scalable information architecture that supports growth without UX debt
- Established reusable principles for enterprise commerce UX

---

## Why This Matters
This work did not just improve a dashboard.
It changed **how enterprise sellers decide what to do next** — with greater confidence, clarity, and speed.
