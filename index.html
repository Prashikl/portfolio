§ V
TenetsFive non-negotiables
i.

Design for trust before speed.

Especially in enterprise and AI-assisted systems, trust is the product. Speed without it produces churn, escalations, and quiet abandonment.

ii.

UX is an operating-model lever.

Not just an interface layer. Decisions here shape ops cost, adoption velocity, and deal quality — long after the screens ship.

iii.

AI accelerates sense-making — it does not replace judgement.

Use it to compress synthesis, draft, and validation. Keep the calls — quality, accountability, ethics — human.

iv.

Optimise for scale, not just usability.

Enterprise systems must work for 1 user and 100,000 users with the same design integrity, the same trust, the same recovery paths.

v.

Secure-by-Design and Responsible AI are architectural, not editorial.

They aren't checklists at the end of a flow. They are constraints I design within from the start.
